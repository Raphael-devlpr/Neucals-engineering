About Neucals

Your Trusted Partner in [Precision Engineering]

Neucals Engineering (Pty) Ltd, based in Boksburg Anderbolt, is a leading provider of comprehensive engineering solutions. We specialize in CNC machining, conventional turning and milling, pump and rotating equipment overhauls, hydraulic cylinder repairs, steel fabrication, welding, and industrial maintenance. Our team serves mining, manufacturing, transport, agricultural, construction, and industrial sectors throughout South Africa.

Our Journey

A [Timeline] of Precision
& Industrial Growth

Foundation Year 2016

Neucals Engineering was established in Boksburg Anderbolt with a mission to deliver high-quality machining and repair services to local industries. Starting with conventional lathes and milling machines, we built a reputation for precision and reliability.

From a small workshop to a trusted name in engineering across Gauteng and beyond.

Expansion: CNC & Hydraulics 2018

Invested in advanced CNC turning and milling centers, enabling high-volume precision component manufacturing. Launched dedicated hydraulic cylinder repair and manufacturing division, serving mining and transport sectors.

Enhanced capabilities in pump repairs and mechanical seal fitting.

Fabrication & Welding Division 2020

Launched dedicated steel fabrication and structural welding unit offering MIG, TIG, and arc welding, custom brackets, frames, trailer manufacturing, and toolbox production.

Became a one-stop shop for engineering and metalwork.

Industrial Maintenance & On-Site Support 2023

Expanded services to include plant maintenance, breakdown repairs, preventative maintenance programs, and on-site engineering support across mining and manufacturing plants.

24/7 emergency response and equipment alignment services added.

Continued Growth & Innovation 2024

Strengthened our position as a leading engineering partner across South Africa, serving mining, manufacturing, transport, agriculture, and construction sectors with quality workmanship and fast turnaround times.

Building the future of industrial engineering.

Looking Ahead: Future Vision

Neucals Engineering continues to invest in advanced machinery, skilled workforce development, and expanded capacity to serve the growing needs of South African industries.

Your partner in precision engineering for years to come.
